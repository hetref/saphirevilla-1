The Perfect Balance of Sophistication & Comfort

Sapphire Villas stands out as a limited-edition community offering tasteful living with BBMP Approved & RERA Registered status (PRM/KA/RERA/1251/446/PR/130924/007016). The project features exclusive 4BHK Villas ranging from 3,500–5,000 sq.ft. with expansive living areas, high-end finishes, natural light with large windows, private gardens, terraces, and balconies.

Each villa is curated for privacy, luxury, and convenience. Modern interiors feature marble flooring for living rooms, wooden floors in master bedrooms, and energy-efficient features like solar water provision. The community offers 40+ world-class amenities including clubhouse, swimming pool, gym, jogging track, tennis courts, and 24x7 security. Located in Sadarmangala, Whitefield, the project is just 5 minutes from top-rated schools, malls, hospitals, and IT Parks with quick access to upcoming Metro and business hubs. Sapphire Villas by 42 Estates offers a peaceful environment, yet well-connected to Bengaluru's vibrant core.
